Remember:

half double crochet: yarn over, put hook through stitch and yarn over, pull through only stitch, yarn over again and pull through all 3 loops on the hook

double crochet: yarn over, put hook through stitch and yarn over, pull through only stitch, yarn over and pull through 2 loops on the hook, yarn over and pull through all 2 loops on the hook

triple crochet: yarn over twice, put hook through next stitch, yarn over and pull only thorugh stitch, yarn over and pull through first 2 loops on hook, yarn over again and pull through first 2 loops on hook, yarn over and pull through remaining loops

increase: do 2 single crochets in the same stitch

decrease: put hook through stitch and yarn over, pull through only the stitch, put hook into next stitch and yarn over, pull through only the stitch, yarn over and go through all 3 loops on the hook
